```
Create a cinematic 8-second perfectly seamless looping video of an American 
semi-truck driving on an interstate highway during golden hour sunset.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
⚠️  CRITICAL LOOPING REQUIREMENT — MOST IMPORTANT:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

This video MUST be a PERFECT SEAMLESS LOOP. 

At exactly 8.00 seconds, the video must return to the EXACT same visual 
state as frame 0.00 — same truck position, same camera angle, same 
background elements, same lighting.

When played on repeat (0→8→0→8→0...), it should look like ONE CONTINUOUS 
INFINITE SHOT with ZERO visible cuts, jumps, or discontinuities.

Think of it like a perfect GIF loop or a "boomerang" effect — the end 
must connect seamlessly back to the beginning. The viewer should NOT be 
able to tell where the video ends and restarts.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
🎥  CAMERA SPECIFICATIONS:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

VIEW ANGLE:
- Follow the truck from behind-right angle (3/4 rear view)
- Camera positioned as if filming from another vehicle
- Slightly elevated perspective (about 6-8 feet high)
- Distance: 100-150 feet behind the truck

CAMERA MOVEMENT:
- Smooth, professional tracking shot
- CONSTANT speed throughout entire 8 seconds (no acceleration/deceleration)
- Perfect stabilization (like on a professional camera rig)
- NO camera shake, NO wobble, NO sudden movements
- The camera must move at EXACTLY the same speed as the truck
- Camera speed at 0:00 = Camera speed at 8:00 (critical for loop)

CAMERA SETTINGS:
- Cinematic depth of field (slight background blur)
- Professional color grading
- Slight film grain (10-15% for texture)
- No lens distortion or fisheye effect

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
🚛  TRUCK SPECIFICATIONS:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

TRUCK MODEL:
- Modern American semi-truck (Freightliner Cascadia or Peterbilt 579)
- 53-foot dry van trailer (standard box trailer)
- Clean, well-maintained appearance
- Professional commercial trucking look
- Realistic road wear and details

TRUCK COLOR:
- Truck cab: Deep blue, red, or classic white
- Trailer: White or light gray (clean, professional)
- Chrome details visible (grille, exhaust stacks)
- Company logo optional (generic or none)

TRUCK POSITION & MOVEMENT:
- Driving in the RIGHT lane (American standard)
- Maintains perfectly steady 65 mph speed
- NO acceleration, NO deceleration, NO speed changes
- NO lane changes, NO turning
- Truck stays centered in frame throughout
- Truck position at 0:00 MUST EQUAL position at 8:00 (critical!)

TRUCK DETAILS:
- Wheels rotating smoothly and realistically
- Slight natural suspension movement (minimal)
- Exhaust visible (subtle smoke/heat shimmer)
- Headlights OFF (daytime/sunset)
- Brake lights OFF (constant speed)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
🛣️  ENVIRONMENT & ROAD:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

HIGHWAY TYPE:
- Wide American interstate highway (I-40, I-80, I-10 style)
- 3-4 lanes visible in frame
- Well-maintained asphalt surface
- Clear lane markings (white dashed lines)
- Smooth, straight road (no curves or hills)

ROAD SURFACE:
- Clean, dry asphalt
- Slight texture visible
- Subtle heat shimmer on distant road (optional)
- No potholes, no construction, no debris

TRAFFIC:
- EMPTY highway — NO other vehicles visible
- This is critical for seamless looping
- No cars passing, no oncoming traffic
- Just the lone truck on an open road

LANDSCAPE:
- American Southwest or Midwest scenery
- Wide open spaces, vast horizons
- Distant mountains or flat plains
- Desert vegetation (cacti, sagebrush) OR grasslands
- Minimal trees (to keep background uniform)
- NO unique landmarks (no signs, no buildings, no bridges)
- Background should be UNIFORM to help with looping

SKY:
- Clear sky with minimal clouds
- Or uniform cloud coverage (no dramatic cloud formations)
- Sky should look the same at 0:00 and 8:00

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
🌅  LIGHTING & ATMOSPHERE:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

TIME OF DAY:
- Golden hour — late afternoon, about 1 hour before sunset
- Sun low on the horizon (15-20 degrees above)
- Warm, golden light bathing the scene

LIGHTING CHARACTERISTICS:
- Warm color temperature (3000-4000K)
- Soft, diffused sunlight
- Long shadows stretching across the road
- Golden and amber tones dominating
- Slight orange glow on truck's side
- Backlit or side-lit (sun from left or right)

LIGHTING CONSISTENCY:
- Lighting MUST remain constant throughout 8 seconds
- 8 seconds is too short for visible sun movement
- No sudden brightness changes
- No flickering or pulsing light
- Consistent shadow direction and length

COLOR GRADING:
- Cinematic color palette
- Slightly desaturated (not overly vibrant)
- Warm tones emphasized (oranges, ambers, golds)
- Cool shadows (subtle blue tints in dark areas)
- Professional film look
- Moody, atmospheric feel

ATMOSPHERE:
- Slight haze in the distance (atmospheric perspective)
- Clean air, excellent visibility
- Peaceful, serene mood
- Nostalgic American highway vibe
- Professional documentary aesthetic

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
🎨  STYLE & AESTHETIC:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

VISUAL STYLE:
- Professional trucking documentary

- Slightly nostalgic, timeless feel
- American road trip aesthetic
- High production value

MOOD & TONE:
- Peaceful and contemplative

- Captures the romance of the open road

FILM REFERENCES:
- American Truck Simulator (game cinematics)

- Modern commercial truck advertisements
- High-end automotive photography

AVOID:
- Amateur or shaky footage
- Overly dramatic or action-movie style
- Cartoon or animated look
- Low quality or blurry footage
- Overly saturated colors
- Fast & Furious style (too dramatic)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
🎬  TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

DURATION:
- EXACTLY 8.0 seconds (not 7.9, not 8.1 — precisely 8.0)
- This exact duration is critical for seamless looping

RESOLUTION:
- 4K (3840 × 2160 pixels)
- Ultra HD quality
- Sharp, crisp details

FRAME RATE:
- 24 fps (cinematic standard)
- Smooth motion blur
- Film-like motion cadence

ASPECT RATIO:
- 16:9 (widescreen standard)
- Horizontal orientation
- Suitable for web and TV display

VIDEO QUALITY:
- High bitrate (minimal compression artifacts)
- Clean, noise-free image (except intentional film grain)
- Professional broadcast quality
- No pixelation or banding

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
🔄  LOOPING STRATEGY & REQUIREMENTS:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

VISUAL CYCLE CONCEPT:
The truck should travel a distance that creates a complete "visual cycle" 
in 8 seconds. This means all moving elements return to their starting 
positions:

TRUCK POSITION:
- At 0.00 seconds: Truck at position A in frame
- At 4.00 seconds: Truck at middle position
- At 8.00 seconds: Truck returns to position A (same as 0.00)

WHEEL ROTATION:
- Wheels should complete an exact number of rotations
- Wheel position at 8.00 = Wheel position at 0.00
- No "jumping" or "teleporting" of wheel spokes

BACKGROUND MOVEMENT:
- Road markings should align at loop point
- Landscape elements should match
- No unique features that break the loop illusion

CAMERA POSITION:
- Camera distance from truck at 0.00 = distance at 8.00
- Camera angle at 0.00 = angle at 8.00
- No drift or deviation in camera tracking

WHAT TO AVOID FOR PERFECT LOOPING:
❌ Other vehicles (they won't be there when loop restarts)
❌ Unique landmarks (signs, buildings, bridges)
❌ Dramatic cloud formations (they'll jump on loop)
❌ Birds or animals crossing
❌ Changing light conditions
❌ Camera speed variations
❌ Truck acceleration or deceleration

LOOPING TEST:
When the video reaches 8.00 seconds and jumps back to 0.00, 
the transition should be INVISIBLE. A viewer watching on repeat 
should not be able to identify the loop point.
```

## ✅ После генерации — Проверка качества

### Шаг 1: Визуальная проверка
```bash
# Открыть в VLC с повтором
vlc Truck_loop.mp4 --loop
```
Смотри 5-10 циклов. Замечаешь стык? Если нет — отлично!

### Шаг 2: Техническая проверка
```bash
# Проверить длительность
ffprobe -v error -show_entries format=duration \
  -of default=noprint_wrappers=1:nokey=1 Truck_loop.mp4

# Должно быть: 8.000000
```

### Шаг 3: Покадровая проверка
```bash
# Извлечь первый и последний кадр
ffmpeg -i Truck_loop.mp4 -vf "select=eq(n\,0)" -vframes 1 frame_first.png
ffmpeg -i Truck_loop.mp4 -sseof -1 -vframes 1 frame_last.png

# Сравнить визуально в Photoshop или любом редакторе
```

## 🛠️ Постобработка (если нужно)

### Если видео не зациклено идеально:

```bash
# Добавить плавный переход между концом и началом
ffmpeg -i input.mp4 -filter_complex \
"[0:v]split[main][dup]; \
[dup]trim=start=7:end=8,setpts=PTS-STARTPTS[end]; \
[main]trim=start=0:end=1,setpts=PTS-STARTPTS[start]; \
[end][start]xfade=transition=fade:duration=0.5:offset=0.5[fade]; \
[main][fade]concat=n=2:v=1[out]" \
-map "[out]" -t 8 output_loop.mp4
```

### Оптимизация для веба:

```bash
# Сжать до 1-2 MB
ffmpeg -i Truck_loop.mp4 \
  -vcodec h264 \
  -crf 28 \
  -preset slow \
  -vf scale=1920:1080 \
  -movflags +faststart \
  Truck_loop_web.mp4

# Конвертировать в WebM (лучше для веба)
ffmpeg -i Truck_loop.mp4 \
  -c:v libvpx-vp9 \
  -b:v 1M \
  -vf scale=1920:1080 \
  Truck_loop.webm
```
