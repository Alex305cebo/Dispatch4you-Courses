What you'll be able to do

The full dispatcher journey — from your first load to repeat clients and steady income

Find profitable loads Load boards, RPM to price the rate, deadhead to pick the route

We'll teach you to work every load board — from the biggest, DAT and Truckstop, to the industry leaders' freight bases. Hundreds of loads post daily and most don't pay. The dispatcher's job is to weed out scams and dead-end offers fast.

  • Run the RPM math — rate per mile including deadhead to the pickup
  • Pick lanes with an easy backhaul and steer clear of dead zones
  • Spot a lowball rate or a scam before you ever dial the broker
Negotiate with brokers Negotiation, broker vetting, rate confirmation — deals on your terms

We'll teach you to negotiate so the rate goes up: a board rate can almost always gain $100–300. You'll learn how a confident dispatcher sounds and why brokers say yes.

  • Back your price with the market, freight type and urgency
  • Vet brokers by credit score and reviews — so you never work for free
  • Read the rate confirmation and catch hidden terms before signing
Run loads without a hitch Check calls, BOL and POD, detention — the run under control

We'll teach you to manage a run from pickup to delivery: when to reach the driver, what to check in the paperwork, and what to do when things go sideways. One slip costs a fine or a broker.

  • Guide the driver through check calls: pickup, in-transit, delivery
  • Keep a clean paper trail — BOL and POD, the documents you get paid on
  • Handle late arrivals, detention, breakdowns and reroutes
Become indispensable Direct brokers, 3–5 trucks running, an industry reputation

We'll teach you to turn one-off runs into steady flow: a dispatcher's income comes from brokers who come back and drivers who trust you — not from the boards.

  • Build broker relationships and get freight direct, off the board
  • Run several trucks in parallel without losing control
  • Work so people recommend you and offer a percentage seat
Get to your first deal 1-on-1 sessions: your cases, first booking, a job

Articles and lessons give you the base — practice gets you the result. In private sessions with a mentor we work on your real loads, all the way to your first booking and a job.

  • We break down your calls and loads, not textbook examples
  • We close your first deal together and paper it right
  • We get you hire-ready: resume, job search, interviews
