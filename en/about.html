Our mission

We built this platform so anyone can master the freight dispatcher profession from scratch — no experience, no fluent English at the start, from anywhere in the world.

The US freight market moves $875 billion a year. A dispatcher coordinates shipments remotely: finding loads, negotiating with brokers, and building efficient routes.
