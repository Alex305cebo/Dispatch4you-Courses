Create an 8-second PERFECTLY SEAMLESS LOOPING cinematic video of a modern 
American semi-truck with DRY VAN BOX TRAILER driving on an empty interstate 
highway during golden hour sunset.

⚠️ CRITICAL REQUIREMENTS:

1. TRAILER TYPE:
   • DRY VAN BOX TRAILER (enclosed rectangular box trailer, 53-foot)
   • NOT a tanker (cylindrical), NOT a flatbed
   • White or gray box trailer with smooth flat sides
   • Looks like a large shipping container on wheels

2. BACKGROUND — MOST IMPORTANT FOR PERFECT LOOPING:
   • UNIFORM flat landscape: Kansas grassland plains OR West Texas low desert
   • NO unique landmarks: NO Monument Valley rocks, NO mountains, NO tall 
     cacti, NO buildings, NO signs
   • Background must be so uniform that when it resets at 8 seconds, the 
     viewer cannot detect any difference
   • Think "endless boring plains" not "scenic landmarks"

3. PERFECT SEAMLESS LOOP:
   • At exactly 8.00 seconds, EVERYTHING returns to exact same position as 
     0.00 seconds
   • Truck position: IDENTICAL at 0:00 and 8:00
   • Background: IDENTICAL at 0:00 and 8:00
   • NO objects should "jump" or "teleport" when looping
   • Transition from 8 sec to 0 sec must be COMPLETELY INVISIBLE
   • When played on repeat (0→8→0→8→0...), it must look like ONE CONTINUOUS 
     INFINITE SHOT

CAMERA:
• Smooth tracking shot from behind-right angle (3/4 rear view)
• Slightly elevated (6-8 feet high), 100-150 feet behind truck
• Perfectly constant speed matching truck
• Professional stabilization, NO shake or wobble
• NO zoom, pan, or tilt

TRUCK:
• Modern American Class 8 semi (Freightliner Cascadia or Peterbilt 579 style)
• Deep metallic blue, classic red, or pearl white
• Driving in right lane at steady 65 mph
• NO speed changes, NO lane changes
• Truck stays centered in frame

HIGHWAY:
• Wide American interstate (3-4 lanes visible)
• Smooth asphalt with white dashed lane markings
• Perfectly straight and flat
• COMPLETELY EMPTY — NO other traffic anywhere

LIGHTING:
• Golden hour — 1 hour before sunset
• Warm golden/orange/amber tones
• Soft diffused sunlight, beautiful rim lighting on truck
• Consistent lighting throughout (NO changes in 8 seconds)

LANDSCAPE EXAMPLES (CHOOSE ONE):
✅ GOOD: Kansas/Nebraska grassland plains (endless flat grass)
✅ GOOD: West Texas low desert (flat with small uniform bushes)
✅ GOOD: Iowa agricultural fields (uniform crop rows)

❌ AVOID: Monument Valley (unique rocks will "jump")
❌ AVOID: Mountains (peaks will "teleport")
❌ AVOID: Tall cacti, buildings, signs (will "jump")

TECHNICAL:
• Duration: EXACTLY 8.0 seconds
• Resolution: 4K (3840×2160)
• Frame rate: 24 fps
• Aspect ratio: 16:9
• Professional cinematic quality

VISUAL STYLE:
• Professional trucking documentary aesthetic
• High-end commercial production
• Cinematic film look with subtle film grain (10-15%)
• American road trip vibe
• Peaceful, contemplative, meditative mood

LOOPING STRATEGY:
The background must be so UNIFORM and REPETITIVE that when the video loops 
back to the beginning, the viewer cannot detect any "jump" or "teleportation" 
of objects.

A simple boring flat landscape that loops perfectly is INFINITELY BETTER 
than beautiful scenic Monument Valley where objects "jump" every 8 seconds.

PRIORITY ORDER:
1. PERFECT SEAMLESS LOOP (most important!)
2. Correct trailer type (dry van box, NOT tanker)
3. Uniform background (flat plains, NOT unique landmarks)
4. Beautiful cinematic quality
5. Professional production
