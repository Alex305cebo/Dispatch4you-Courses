📋 СКОПИРУЙ ЭТОТ ПРОМПТ ЦЕЛИКОМ И ВСТАВЬ В GEMINI:

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Create an 8-second PERFECTLY SEAMLESS LOOPING cinematic video of an 
American semi-truck with DRY VAN BOX TRAILER driving on an interstate 
highway during golden hour sunset.

⚠️ CRITICAL REQUIREMENTS:

1. TRAILER TYPE:
   - DRY VAN BOX TRAILER (enclosed rectangular box trailer)
   - NOT a tanker, NOT a flatbed
   - Standard 53-foot white/gray box trailer
   - Looks like a large rectangular shipping container on wheels

2. BACKGROUND — MOST IMPORTANT FOR LOOPING:
   - UNIFORM flat landscape (Kansas-style grassland plains)
   - OR low desert with small uniform bushes (West Texas style)
   - NO unique landmarks (NO Monument Valley rock formations!)
   - NO mountains, NO tall cacti, NO buildings, NO signs
   - Background must be so uniform that when it "resets" at 8 seconds,
     the viewer cannot tell the difference

3. PERFECT LOOP:
   - At exactly 8.00 seconds, EVERYTHING must return to exact same 
     position as 0.00 seconds
   - Truck position: IDENTICAL at 0:00 and 8:00
   - Background: IDENTICAL at 0:00 and 8:00
   - NO objects should "jump" or "teleport" when looping
   - When played on repeat (0→8→0→8→0...), it must look like ONE 
     CONTINUOUS INFINITE SHOT

CAMERA:
- Smooth tracking shot from behind-right angle (3/4 rear view)
- Constant speed throughout (no acceleration/deceleration)
- Professional stabilization

TRUCK MOVEMENT:
- Perfectly steady 65 mph speed
- Stays in right lane
- NO speed changes, NO lane changes

ENVIRONMENT:
- Wide empty interstate highway (3-4 lanes)
- FLAT uniform landscape extending to horizon
- Clear weather
- NO other traffic

LIGHTING:
- Golden hour sunset
- Warm orange/amber tones
- Consistent lighting throughout (8 seconds is too short for sun movement)
- Cinematic color grading

TECHNICAL:
- Duration: EXACTLY 8.0 seconds
- Resolution: 4K (3840×2160)
- Frame rate: 24 fps
- Aspect ratio: 16:9

LOOPING STRATEGY:
Think of this as creating an "infinite highway" animation. The background 
should be so uniform and repetitive that when it loops back to the 
beginning, the viewer cannot detect any "jump" or "teleportation" of 
objects.

Simplicity and uniformity are MORE IMPORTANT than scenic beauty.

A boring flat landscape that loops perfectly is BETTER than beautiful 
Monument Valley scenery that "jumps" every 8 seconds.

1. DRY VAN = прямоугольный закрытый фургон (НЕ цистерна!)
