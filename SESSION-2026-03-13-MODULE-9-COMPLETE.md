### Сектор 2: Roadside Inspections
**Контент:**
- Что такое roadside inspection
- 6 типов inspections (Level 1-6)
- Level 1 Full Inspection - самый полный (30-60 минут, проверка всего)
- Pre-trip inspection (30 минут каждое утро)
- Документы в траке (CDL, medical card, registration, insurance, IFTA, logbook, DVIR, BOL)
- Правила поведения во время inspection
- Результаты: clean inspection, violations, Out-of-Service

**Case Study:** Clean inspection спас $10,000 контракт с Amazon

**Квиз:** Какой самый полный тип roadside inspection? (Ответ: Level 1)

### Сектор 3: Out-of-Service Violations
**Контент:**
- Что такое Out-of-Service (критическое нарушение)
- Топ-10 OOS violations (brakes 20%+, tires <4/32", lights, steering, suspension, HOS 11+ hours, license, medical card, drug/alcohol, cargo securement)
- Штрафы за OOS ($1,000-25,000)
- Что делать при OOS (call dispatcher, find mechanic, fix problem, get clearance)
- Что НЕ делать (drive while OOS = federal crime)
- Статистика: 20% inspections заканчиваются OOS

**Case Study:** OOS violation стоил $8,000 и 24 часа (brake violation 25%)

**Квиз:** Какое самое частое OOS violation? (Ответ: Brake system violations 30%)

### Сектор 4: Программы безопасности
**Контент:**
- Зачем нужны safety programs (lower CSA, fewer accidents, lower insurance)
- Driver training programs (new driver orientation, road test, mentor program, HOS training, quarterly meetings)
- Maintenance programs (A-Service 10K-15K miles, B-Service 25K-30K miles, C-Service 50K-60K miles)
- DVIR (Daily Vehicle Inspection Report) - обязательно каждый день
- Driver monitoring (ELD, dash cams, GPS tracking, telematics)
- Safety incentive programs ($100-500/месяц за 0 violations)
- Safety policies (drug/alcohol, distracted driving, speeding, fatigue management)

**Case Study:** Safety program сэкономил $100,000 в год (dash cams, training, speed limiters, incentives)

**Квиз:** Как часто нужно делать A-Service? (Ответ: Каждые 10,000-15,000 миль)

---

### Сектор 5: Предотвращение аварий
**Контент:**
- Defensive driving techniques (space management 7-8 секунд, scan ahead, check mirrors, anticipate hazards)
- Following distance (7 секунд dry, 10-12 wet, 15-20 snow/ice)
- Driving in weather conditions (rain, snow/ice, fog, high winds)
- Fatigue management (признаки усталости, power nap 15-20 минут, что НЕ работает)
- Accident protocols (stop, check injuries, call 911, set triangles, call dispatcher, exchange info, photos, police report, drug test)
- Статистика: 30% аварий - driver fatigue, 23% - speeding, 15% - distracted driving

**Case Study:** Defensive driving предотвратил $500,000 аварию (снег, гололед, 4-car pileup)

**Квиз:** Какая безопасная дистанция следования в сухих условиях? (Ответ: 7-8 секунд)

---

### Сектор 6: Проверка брокеров
**Контент:**
- Зачем проверять брокеров (non-payment, delayed payment, fake loads, double brokering)
- Инструменты проверки:
  - FMCSA SAFER (FREE) - MC# validity, authority, insurance
  - RTS Pro/Carrier411 ($30-50/месяц) - credit score A/B/C/D/F, payment history
  - Google search (FREE) - reviews, complaints, BBB rating
  - LinkedIn check (FREE) - company page, employees
- 5-минутный чек-лист проверки брокера
- Red flags (no MC#, inactive authority, credit score D/F, payment 60+ days, upfront payment, pressure to book, Gmail email)
- Green flags (MC# 2+ years, credit score A/B, payment 30 days, written rate confirmation, professional communication)
- Вопросы брокеру (MC#, payment terms, factoring, rate confirmation, deductions)
- Документы для защиты (rate confirmation, signed BOL, POD, lumper receipt, detention time, photos)

**Case Study:** 5-минутная проверка спасла $8,000 (broker credit score F, scam)

**Квиз:** Какой credit score брокера считается хорошим? (Ответ: A или B)

---

### Сектор 7: Защита от мошенничества
**Контент:**
- Типы мошенничества:
  - Freight fraud (кража груза $200K-500K, fake carrier, electronics/pharmaceuticals)
  - Identity theft (кража MC#, fake documents)
  - Double brokering (illegal, broker B перепродает груз carrier C)
  - Fake load scams (upfront payment $200-500, груз не существует)
- Как защититься (verify broker MC#, check credit score, call broker back, verify shipper, get everything in writing)
- Best practices (work with known brokers, use reputable load boards, factoring company, trust your gut, document everything)
- Что делать если стали жертвой fraud (stop communication, document, report to FMCSA/FBI/police, notify insurance, legal action)
- Cybersecurity (strong passwords, 2FA, secure email, VPN, antivirus, backup data, phishing awareness)

**Case Study:** Freight fraud стоил $350,000 (Apple products, fake carrier, украденный MC#)

**Квиз:** Какой главный red flag при freight fraud? (Ответ: Новый MC# + pressure to book immediately)
